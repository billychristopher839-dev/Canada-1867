About this game

Dominion 1867 is a trivia role-playing game. You play the Government of Canada, starting at Confederation on July 1, 1867. Answer quiz questions on Canadian history, economy, and politics; every 2 correct answers accomplishes 1 milestone — 311 real, documented policies and events across all 24 Prime Ministers.

Answer wrongly and the question returns to the order paper later — nothing is skipped, everything must eventually pass the House. When all 622 questions are answered and all 311 milestones accomplished, the story is complete: Canada stands first in the world in Quality of Life and the Human Development Index.
